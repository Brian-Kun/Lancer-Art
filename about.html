Hello! I'm Brian Ramirez

I am a 17 year-old senior at Lawrence High School, Math Science and Technology. I created this website with the purpose of showcasing all the talented students in Lawrence High School. All these works of art are worthy of being shared with the world, so I thought that a website would be the perfect means of communication in order to make this possible. The website itself is a piece of art; I worked for several months on the concept and code of this website. I hope you enjoy it and share it with the whole world.
